Hot Dog

Description

Classic amongst the classics. When nothing works go for a "chien chaud".

Ingredients
  • Hot dog bun
  • Sausage
  • Condiments of your choice
Steps
  1. Toast the bun
  2. Cook the sausage either by boiling or grilling
  3. Assemble everything with your condiments. Eat immediately for maximum taste. Regret 10 min later.
