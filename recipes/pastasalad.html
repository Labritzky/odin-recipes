Summertime Pasta Salad

Description

This healthy pasta salad features bold summer flavors, including tomatoes, corn and black beans tossed in spicy, fresh pesto! Itâ€™s a great vegetarian side dish for potlucks and barbecues.

Ingredients
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Black beans
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh corn
  • Feta
  • Jalapeno pesto
Steps
  1. Prepare boiling water for pasta, keep some cooking water.
  2. Once the pasta are cooked, mix with pesto and a splash of cooking water and toss well.
  3. Transfer the pasta to a serving bowl and add the black beans, cherry tomatoes and feta.
  4. stir until combined. Tastes better if you let it marinated for 30 minutes but feel free to enjoy it now.
